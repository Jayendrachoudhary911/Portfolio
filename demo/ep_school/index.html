About Us

Who we are really?

Engineer's Point Senior Secondary School in Khairthal, Alwar District, Rajasthan, is a co-educational institution offering both English and Hindi medium instruction from Nursery to Grade 12. The school follows the CBSE curriculum and provides specialized coaching for competitive exams such as IIT-JEE, NEET, NTSE, Olympiads, and CA. With a commitment to building bright futures, Engineer's Point emphasizes a comprehensive education that prepares students for academic and professional success.
